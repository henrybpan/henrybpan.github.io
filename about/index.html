I hate when people say: "tell me about yourself." What do you want to know?

My age? My ethnicity? My religion? My interests? My job?

I could give you the answers to those questions but you still wouldn't know who I am. That's because I, like you and every other person in the world, cannot be described by an amalgamation of nouns, adjectives, and adverbs. I am not ____, and neither are you. We are a collection of experiences, conversations, and impacts.

For that reason I find that the best way for people to know who I am is to watch my videos or read my essays.[1]

And if you are looking for a more concrete answer to this question:

I am a 19 year-old 2nd gen Chinese American Christian content creator. I'm also 6'0 ft tall, rich, and single.[2] I'm interested in Christian ethics, aesthetics, and behavioral economics. I am trying to become a better writer, and read more books.
