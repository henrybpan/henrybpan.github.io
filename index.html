About

I'm 18 years old, building at the intersection of faith and ambition. First-year at the University of Virginia. Founding UVA's first space for content creators. Coaching Christian entrepreneurs on personal branding.

I grew up sheltered, with every advantage. Private schools, safety nets, a family that gave me everything. And somewhere in that comfort I realized — I need to build something that's mine. Not to leave my family behind, but to stand next to them as someone who earned his own ground.

I believe we must approach the world with childlike optimism while understanding that no one is coming to save us. We have everything we need. Most people never even start.

"Seek ye first the kingdom of God, and his righteousness; and all these things shall be added unto you."

— Matthew 6:33
Thoughts
01
February 2026
Because of the internet our joy has been snatched away and replaced with informed cynicism. We have to forget all of that — forget all the existential noise that limits us — and create and imagine like we did when we were young. Back then we believed anything was possible, and somewhere along the way we forgot that, but it never stopped being true.
02
January 2026
Lack of long-term success is completely due to one's inability to act and iterate. If you suck at something, it is because you don't want it enough. There is no other excuse.
03
January 2026
Never be satisfied with what you have, but always be grateful for it. These are not contradictions. Gratitude without ambition is complacency. Ambition without gratitude is vanity.
