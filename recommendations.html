Recommendations

The Founders Podcast

If I could only listen to one podcast for the rest of my life it would be this. Michael Dell, Ken Griffin, and Tiger Woods are among my favorite episodes. More thoughts to come.

High Agency by George Mack

This essay changed my life. The concept seems obvious, and is, but it was something that had never been fully articulated to me as an 18 year old. This essay was one of the many factors that ignited my journey in content creation and my overall pursuit of success.

Feathered Indians by Tyler Childers

While you might have never looked over West Virginia, smoking Spirits on the roof, we will all experience a time where our partner will disapprove of our habits. Hearing Childers's response to his partner's disapproval makes me imagine what type of person I'd have to meet for me to leave certain things behind.
