Questions

Do I need to know how to code?

No. The Two-Call tier exists because you don't. I run the install while you watch. The Prompts tier assumes you can follow a Loom video and paste a few commands into a terminal. Still not coding.

What if something breaks after you leave?

Claude Code is your support. The whole system is built so you talk to Claude like you'd talk to me. I also cover the common failure modes on the second call so you're not stuck.

Does this work on Windows?

Not yet. Mac (Apple Silicon) only. The transcription model runs on your GPU locally and I'm not shipping a half-working Windows version at this price. On the roadmap, not promised.

How is this different from ChatGPT or Jasper?

ChatGPT forgets you every session. Jasper knows templates, not your content. This thing reads every reel you've made, holds it forever, and writes back in your voice. It's yours. Nothing leaves your machine.

Is my Instagram data safe?

Yes. Whole system runs on your computer. Your token, your transcripts, your brand docs — none of it touches a server you don't own. I see it once during the install and never again.

Do I need a Claude subscription?

You need a paid Claude plan to run Claude Code. That's Anthropic's, not mine. Starts at $20/mo. The $100/mo Updates tier is optional and separate — that's how you get every new template and skill I ship.
