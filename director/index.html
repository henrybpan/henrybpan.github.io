Questions

What actually happens after I pay?

DFY: you'll land on Calendly, pick a time for call 1, and I'll email you a pre-install checklist. First call runs 60–90 min — we install together, ingest your last 30 reels, build your voice profile live. Call 2 is a few days later for training.

DWY: you'll land on a thank-you page, drop your GitHub username, and within 24 hours I add you to the private repo and email you the Loom walkthrough. From there you run it at your pace — Claude handles the questions.

Do I need to know how to code?

No. DFY exists because you don't — I run the install while you watch. DWY assumes you can follow a Loom and paste a few commands into a terminal. Still not coding.

Do I keep the system if I stop paying the retainers?

Yes. Whatever you cloned is yours forever. Your current version keeps working on your machine — nothing I ship breaks what you already have.

Here's the mechanic: when you cancel the updates retainer, I remove you as a collaborator on the private repo. That means you stop pulling new skills, templates, and platform expansions as I ship them. But your local copy doesn't disappear, phone home, or expire. No DRM, no kill switch. Cancel clean, keep what you built.

What if something breaks after you leave?

Claude Code is your support. The whole system is built so you talk to Claude like you'd talk to me. Common failure modes are covered on call 2. For everything else, the tech support retainer ($50/mo) is there if you want a real person.

Does this work on Windows?

Not yet. Mac (Apple Silicon) only. The transcription model runs locally on your GPU, and I'm not shipping a half-working Windows version at this price. Roadmap, not promise.

How is this different from ChatGPT or Jasper?

ChatGPT forgets you every session. Jasper knows templates, not your content. This thing reads every reel you've made, holds it forever, and writes back in your voice. Nothing leaves your machine.

Is my Instagram data safe?

Yes. The whole system runs on your computer. Your token, your transcripts, your brand docs — none of it touches a server you don't own. I see it once during the install and never again.

Do I need a Claude subscription?

You need a paid Claude plan to run Claude Code. That's Anthropic's, not mine. Starts at $20/mo.
