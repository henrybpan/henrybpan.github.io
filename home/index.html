About Me!

I got rejected from every single one of my dream colleges. I thought I was a failure.

Then I discovered content creation and building a personal brand. It was the first thing I felt truly passionate about.

I grew from 0 to 30,000 followers in just 3 months.

Now, I help aspiring and ambitious creators unlock their passion for content creation and grow their personal brands. I've been where you are, and I know exactly what it takes to break through.

Through my coaching program, I've helped creators go from stuck at zero to building engaged communities of thousands. My approach focuses on authentic storytelling, building confidence on camera, and creating systems that work—without burning out.

When I'm not coaching or creating content, you'll find me exploring new ideas, reading, and constantly learning how to become better at my craft.
