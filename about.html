About

Hi! I'm Henry.

I'm 18 years old and I'm currently a first year student at UVA.

Ever since I was young I knew I wanted to make a name for myself. The thing was, I thought I was stupid. I always compared myself to the people in my class that were really talented at art, writing, or coding. I tried to make up for my perceived lack of talent by working hard, getting good grades, and grinding extracurriculars. But all that hard work only resulted in college rejections, burned relationships, and a rather forgettable high school experience.

After graduating, I sat in my bedroom listening to podcasts on the lives of great entrepreneurs. I thought to myself, "I want to be great like them." However, I still had no idea what I wanted to do. That was when I discovered content creation. It began as a project for me to share what I had learned from my month of learning from the founders of old, but I quickly realized that nobody was going to take business advice from an 18 year old, especially one who had created nothing in his life.

In the next months, I shifted my content. I stopped trying to give advice to others and began documenting my path to being a better person. Along that path I converted to Christianity, broke up with my girlfriend, and grew 30K followers (in no particular order).

Now in my first year at UVA, I opened up my coaching, started a club, took philosophy "for fun" (big mistake), made tons of friends, and learned the true impossibility of work-life balance.

I'm not great yet, but I continue to strive every day.
