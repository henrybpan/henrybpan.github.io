Thoughts

February 2026

Because of the internet our joy has been snatched away and replaced with informed cynicism. We have to forget all of that — forget all the existential noise that limits us — and create and imagine like we did when we were young. Back then we believed anything was possible, and somewhere along the way we forgot that, but it never stopped being true.

January 2026

Lack of long-term success is completely due to one's inability to act and iterate. If you suck at something, it is because you don't want it enough. There is no other excuse.

January 2026

Never be satisfied with what you have, but always be grateful for it. These are not contradictions. Gratitude without ambition is complacency. Ambition without gratitude is vanity.
